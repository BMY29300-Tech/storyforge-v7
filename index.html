🎨 Google Flow Guide (Nano Banana)

Generate 20 scene images — 20 free tries

📋 Step-by-Step Workflow

  1. Export Image Prompts: Go to Video Prompts → Select Episode → Download "Image Prompts TXT"
  2. Open Google Flow: Go to aistudio.google.com → Sign in with curiousrama.official@gmail.com
  3. Copy Scene 1 Prompt: Open downloaded TXT, copy Scene 1 image prompt
  4. Paste to Flow: Paste prompt with prefix: "Pixar 3D style, 2K, 16:9, " + [prompt]
  5. Generate: Click Generate → Wait ~30 seconds → Download image
  6. Save File: Name as scene_01.png, scene_02.png... scene_20.png
  7. Repeat: Do this for all 20 scenes (you have 20 free tries)
  8. Organize: Keep all images in folder: EP02/images/

🤖 Grok Video Guide

Generate 20 scene videos — 20 free tries

📋 Step-by-Step Workflow

  1. Export Video Prompts: Go to Video Prompts → Select Episode → Download "Video Prompts TXT"
  2. Open Grok: Go to x.com/grok or use Grok mobile app
  3. Copy Scene 1 Prompt: Open downloaded TXT, copy Scene 1 video prompt
  4. Paste to Grok: Paste with prefix: "Generate 6 second video, Pixar 3D style, 2K, 16:9" + [prompt]
  5. Generate: Click Generate → Wait ~60 seconds → Download video
  6. Save File: Name as scene_01.mp4, scene_02.mp4... scene_20.mp4
  7. Repeat: Do this for all 20 scenes (you have 20 free tries)
  8. Organize: Keep all videos in folder: EP02/videos/
